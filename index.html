They say a leader's true nature is revealed in the storm. As a mountaineer and global program/project manager, I have learned that the mountain and large-scale tech projects share the same golden rule: success isn't just about reaching the summit — it is about knowing how to dance with the unpredictability of the weather — and the market.

Não vendo apenas tecnologia — vendo a resolução de problemas complexos que melhoram a vida das pessoas. With over 30 years of international experience (Schréder, Itron, Silver Spring Networks), I have built my career at the intersection of Commercial Strategy, Project Management and Solution Engineering.
